About Me

I’m Bardya, the son of immigrant parents who moved to Bellevue, WA, when I was young. Growing up in a diverse environment taught me resilience and gave me a deep appreciation for community. I earned my degree in Chemical Engineering and started my professional journey working in technical sales alongside scientists and engineers.

But I soon realized I wanted my career to directly impact as many lives as possible. My passion for food, travel, and culture led me around the globe, opening my eyes to the unique challenges restaurant owners face daily. Small businesses, particularly restaurants, are the heart of communities—and they have it tougher than ever.

I’m new to Toast and I don’t claim to have all the answers. But I’m genuinely curious, deeply passionate, and driven by a sincere desire to support local restaurants in and around Olympia. I personally built this website from scratch—over 30 hours and 5,000 lines of code—not outsourced, no agencies. Just me, driven by the same energy and dedication I plan to bring to every single customer.

If I’ve put this much care into our first introduction, imagine how committed I’ll be to your success.

Outside work, you can find me capturing moments through photography—mostly food, nature, and travel. Feel free to explore my photography journey on Instagram @yourhandle.
