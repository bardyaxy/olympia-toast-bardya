About Me

Hey, I'm Bardya. I moved to the U.S. at 16 and settled in Bellevue, WA. Adjusting to a new life and completely different culture as a teenager shaped me. It taught me resilience, openness, and a deep appreciation for tight knit communities.

I earned my degree in Chemical Engineering and spent my early professional life in technical sales, working closely with scientists and engineers. But I felt something was missing. I wanted to make a direct impact, something I could clearly see and feel.

My passion for food, travel, and culture has led me to countless restaurants around the world, seeing firsthand the daily challenges owners face. Small businesses like yours are truly the heartbeat of our neighborhoods, and today, they're facing tougher times than ever.

I'm new to Toast, but I'm bringing genuine curiosity and a personal drive to help Olympia's restaurant community thrive. This entire website you're on right now? I built it myself. It took over 50 hours and more than 5,000 lines of code without any outsourcing, agency help, or even direct assistance from Toast. It’s important to me because this is exactly how committed I’ll be to you and your restaurant's success.

When I'm not working, you'll probably find me behind my camera, capturing food, nature, and adventures from my travels. I'd love to share those moments with you. Check out my photography journey on Instagram @Bardyabani.
